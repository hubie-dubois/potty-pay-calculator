"Clear pee always means perfect hydration."

Not always. Pale yellow is commonly considered a healthy target for many people.

"Holding it forever is harmless."

Regularly delaying bathroom trips can be uncomfortable and is not a great long-term habit.

"Everyone has the same bowel rhythm."

Normal frequency varies a lot. Consistency and comfort matter more than a single number.

"Coffee is only chaos."

Caffeine can stimulate the gut for some people, but responses differ person to person.
